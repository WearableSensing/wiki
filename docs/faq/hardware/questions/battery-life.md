# What is the battery life of the devices?

The battery life of Wearable Sensing devices varies by model:

## DSI-24
- **Configuration:** Two hot-swappable batteries
- **Battery Life:** 8-12 hours per battery
- **Hot-Swap:** Continue recording without interruption while replacing batteries
- **Charging:** Removable batteries charge in a few hours with provided charger

## DSI-7
- **Configuration:** Single battery
- **Battery Life:** Over 12 hours continuous use
- **Charging:** Removable battery charges in a few hours with provided charger

## DSI-VR300
- **Configuration:** Built-in battery
- **Battery Life:** 8-12 hours
- **Charging:** Charges via USB cable in a few hours

## DSI-Flex
- **Configuration:** Built-in rechargeable battery
- **Battery Life:** 8-12 hours
- **Charging:** Charges via USB cable in a few hours

## DSI-EEG + fNIRS
- **Configuration:** Built-in rechargeable battery
- **Battery Life:** 4 hours
- **Charging:** Charges via provided charger in a few hours
