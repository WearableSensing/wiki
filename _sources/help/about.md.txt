## Our Technology

Wearable Sensing is revolutionizing neuroscience research and enabling innovative applications across various commercial domains. These include:

- Neurorehabilitation
- Brain computer interface (BCI)
- Virtual reality
- Neurofeedback
- Neuromarketing
- Neuroeducation
- And many more
